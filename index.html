Maximilian Bernkopf

Math Geek / Data Nerd

Max finished his PhD in computational mathematics at the TU Wien focussing on the numerics of time-harmonic wave propagation problems.

Besides mathematics, he is a true data nerd. He loves handling and visualizing big data sets and discovering the unique stories they can tell.

His languages of choice are R and Python, depending on the task at hand.

He gained his data science experience working at start-ups in Vienna, where he solved problems involving time series analysis and outlier detection.
